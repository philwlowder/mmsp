A successful concert

post: 2/24/2016

Approximately 80 people enjoyed the Mac Johnson Concert at Morrow Mountain State Park Lodge last Sunday afternoon. Mac performed John Denver selections for nearly two hours. He came from a long line of musicians, but says the music of John Denver was hugely influential and inspiring.
