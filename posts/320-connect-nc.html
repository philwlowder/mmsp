NC Connect

post: 2/26/2016

At the NC Friends of State Parks Board Meeting at Haw River State Park the board approved and asked all local Friends groups to strongly support the $75 million dollar bond effort going directly to the state parks. Vote yes on March 15th! Failure to pass these bonds on March 15th would prove to be a huge problem for the NC state park system.

1. The Connect NC Bonds provide a very important $75 million to NC State Parks.

2. The Connect NC Bonds provide $1,537,500 to Morrow Mountain State Park for additional visitor cabins.

3. The Connect NC Bonds also provide $5,510,980 for Stanly Community College.
