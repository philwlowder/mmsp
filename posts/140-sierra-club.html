Sierra Club Celebration

posted: 8/23/2014

Save the Date!


Please join the NC Sierra Club in
Celebration of the 50th Anniversary of the Wilderness Act!

Friday September 26 - Saturday September 27
Morrow Mountain State Park
Albemarle, NC

Activities include canoeing, hiking, service, birding, fishing, star-gazing
and a special guided tour of the Kron restoration.

Celebratory dinner Saturday at 5:30pm at the Morrow Mountain Lodge
Barbeque and fixin's or Vegetarian Lasagna
Mixed Green Salad
Wilderness Birthday Cake

Program Park Ranger Ron Anundson on the History of Morrow Mtn. St Pk
Keynote Speaker - Brent Martin, The Wilderness Society
Backcountry Travelogue with Jerry Weston

Watch for registration details in August Footnotes Online!
Contact Nancy Card, OurWildNC@gmail.com, 910-540-3088
