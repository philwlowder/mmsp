"Introduction to Canoeing"
Taught by Ranger Jason Murvine an American Canoe Association instructor.

Sat, July 25, 2015
Morrow Mountain State Park ----- (704) 982-4402
YOU MUST REGISTER IN ADVANCE FOR THIS CLASS AS SPACE IS LIMITED

We will cover basic paddling strokes and what to do when you flip over.

The park will supply boats, pfds, and paddles.

What to bring?
  • Sunscreen, sunglasses, and a hat
  • Wear clothing you plan to get wet in and your feet must have shoes or sandals on them
  • Water bottle full of water
